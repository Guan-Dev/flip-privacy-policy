Privacy Policy for Master Flipper

Last updated: July 24, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Master Flipper ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "Service").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information We Do NOT Collect

Master Flipper is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, including:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Gameplay data or statistics
  • Any other personal data

2.2 Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Game progress and level completion status
  • Game settings (sound, music, vibration preferences)
  • High scores and statistics

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Advertising

Our app may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google AdMob may collect and use data in accordance with their privacy policy. Please review Google's privacy policy at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3.2 App Stores

When you download our app from app stores (Google Play Store, Apple App Store), those platforms may collect information according to their respective privacy policies.

4. Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit personal data, there are no data security risks associated with our app's data handling. All game data is stored locally on your device and protected by your device's security measures.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages, including children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal information, we comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide.

6. Data Deletion

To delete all game data, simply uninstall the app from your device. This will remove all locally stored game progress, settings, and statistics.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete from our systems. All your game data remains under your control on your device.
